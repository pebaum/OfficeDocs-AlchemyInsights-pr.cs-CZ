Další informace najdete v tématu [Platba předplatného Office 365 pro firmy](https://support.office.com/article/734f4aab-df2d-4e9b-8cb1-691910bde216).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="16aac-p101">The option to pay by invoice is only available if your Office 365 subscription costs over a certain amount (which varies by service location). 2. <span data-ttu-id="16aac-p102">Vyberte předplatné, které chcete změnit.
